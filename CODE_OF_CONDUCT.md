# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and welcoming experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus disagreements on ideas and implementation details.
- Respect maintainers' time and project scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Spam or intentionally disruptive participation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project community.
